What is the Stock Market?

The Stock Market is the location where investors can buy and sell investments into public companies and corporations. This process allows investors to gain a share of ownership of the company. There are several different types of stocks which investors can invest in. The goal of investors when buying and selling stocks are to turn a profit through the movement of the stock prices.

Why invest?

Investors use their personal money to finance a company, with the intent of creating profit from an incline of the stock price. The benefit of investing money into the Stock Market is the possibility of growing the money without having to do too much work.
